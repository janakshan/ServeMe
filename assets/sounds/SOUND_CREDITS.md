- **buttonTap.mp3**: Electronic click sound for UI interactions
- **correct.mp3**: Positive mouth sound for correct answers
- **wrong.mp3**: Negative mouth sound for wrong answers  
- **levelUp.mp3**: Chiptune jingle for achievements and streaks
- **celebration.mp3**: Orchestra sound for exam completion
