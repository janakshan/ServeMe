## Education Service Enhancements

### 1. AI-Powered Study Assistant

**Description**: Intelligent chatbot that helps students with questions, explanations, and study guidance.

**Features**:
- **24/7 Study Help**: Ask questions anytime and get instant responses
- **Concept Explanations**: Break down complex topics into simple explanations
- **Problem Solving**: Step-by-step guidance for math and science problems
- **Study Tips**: Personalized study strategies based on learning style
- **Voice Interaction**: Ask questions using voice commands
- **Multi-Language Support**: Respond in Tamil, Sinhala, or English

**Benefits**:
- Immediate help without waiting for teachers
- Reduces learning barriers
- Increases student confidence
- Available outside class hours

**Description**: Dynamic commission structure with performance-based incentives.

**Features**:
- **Tiered Commissions**: Higher volume = lower commission
- **Performance Bonuses**: Incentives for top providers
- **Promotional Periods**: Reduced commission for new providers
- **Category-Based Rates**: Different rates per service type
- **Peak Hour Pricing**: Dynamic pricing during high demand
- **Loyalty Discounts**: Long-term provider benefits
- **Transparent Dashboard**: Clear commission tracking

**Benefits**:
- Optimized profitability
- Provider retention
- Market competitiveness
- Flexible business model

**Implementation Priority**: Low
**Estimated Effort**: 3-4 weeks
