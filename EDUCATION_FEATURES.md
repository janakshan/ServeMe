## Overview

The Education Service is a comprehensive e-learning platform designed specifically for students in Sri Lanka, covering education from Primary level through Advanced Level (A/L) examination preparation. The platform features a beautiful purple academic theme and provides an immersive learning experience with interactive features, progress tracking, and competitive elements.

### Target Audience
- **Primary Students**: Grades 1-5
- **Junior Secondary Students**: Grades 6-8
- **O/L Students**: Preparing for Ordinary Level examinations
- **A/L Students**: Preparing for Advanced Level examinations

### Core Philosophy
- **Student-Centered Learning**: Everything designed with the student's learning journey in mind
- **Progress Visibility**: Clear tracking of achievements and areas for improvement
- **Engagement**: Gamification elements to keep students motivated
- **Quality Content**: Curated courses from experienced educators
